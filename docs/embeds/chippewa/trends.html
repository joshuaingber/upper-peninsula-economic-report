Employment & Salary Trends

Between 2019 and 2025, employment in Chippewa went from 11,934 to 12,597, an increase of 5.6%. Average annual wages went from $39,520 to $62,400, rising 57.9% over the same period.

Source: BLS QCEW — Quarterly
